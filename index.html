Street Fighter

Street fighter v2.0

Street fighter v1.7 ai only, turbo speed, low hp, fast match

Make sure to mouse click/tap on the loading text/javascript stage to activate it otherwise it will not actually load

2 player mode available with gamepads

Controls:
- Gamepad enabled, Xbox, ps4/ps5, etc

- Mobile: Four finger tap the screen once it has loaded. Only ai fights avail

Keyboard

- 1: Start game in ai fight
- 2: Start game single player. In game, use to pause game

- Wads: Move left, right, crouch, jump
- < : Punch
- > : Kick
- Move away from opponent to block

Special moves
- Down, Forward, Punch: Hadouken(Fireball)
- Forward, Down, Forward, Punch: Shoryuken(spinning uppercut)
- Down, Back, Kick: Tatsumaki Senpukyaku(spinning kick)
- 1 Special Meter cost: Down, Forward, Down, Forward Punch: Super Hadouken(fireball)
- 1 Special Meter cost: Down, Back, Down, Back, Kick: Super Tatsumaki Senpukyaku(spinning kick)

Tetris

Tetris v3.2 recconmmended for desktop play(uses more screen)

Testirs v5.3 reccommended for mobile play. Tap the colored squares at the bottom for controls

Controls
- A: Move piece left
- D: Move piece right
- S/↓: Drop piece/Confirm bottom position
- ←: Rotate piece left
- →: Rotate piece right
- ↑: Hold piece/switch to held piece
- R: Start game/Restart game


Mobile Controls

-Blue left: Rotate piece left
-Blue Right: Rotate piece right
-Red left: Move piece Left
-Red Right: Move piece Right
-Purple: Hold piece/switch to held piece
-Yellow: Drop piece/Confirm bottom position

Pacman

Pacman

Eat as many dots as you can, ghosts create dots so can you get the remaining dots to 0? Touching ghosts lowers your score. If you have a power pallet, ghosts get stunned and cannot create dots.

Controls

Wads: Move left, right, up and down

5: Start/restart proper level with ghosts
1-4: Start different level layout with no real purpose

+: Increase game speed, 4-7 reccommended. Increase high up to watch ghosts refill the play area with dots or create extra power pallets!
-: Decrease game speed

Mobile

5 finger tap: begin game

3 fingers on screen and tap with a fourth finger: increase game speed
2 fingers on screen and tap with a third finger: decrease game speed

Tap left of screen: move left
Tap right of screen: move right
Tap upper screen: move up
Tap bottom of screen: move down

Smash13

Gamepad or mobile only
2 player mode available
